About Me

Instructional Designer and eLearning professional focused on creating engaging learner-centered digital learning experiences.

I am an Instructional Designer and eLearning professional with experience in creating learner-centered digital learning experiences for K–12 education and training environments.

My background includes instructional design, LMS administration, curriculum coordination, and interactive eLearning development using tools such as Articulate Storyline 360 and Rise 360.

I enjoy transforming learning content into engaging digital experiences through storytelling, interaction design, multimedia integration, and structured learning flows.

I have experience working with SCORM-based learning modules, LMS testing and deployment, learner engagement strategies, and collaborative content development with subject matter experts.

I am particularly interested in interactive learning design, microlearning experiences, scenario-based learning, and creating impactful educational content that combines creativity with instructional strategy.
